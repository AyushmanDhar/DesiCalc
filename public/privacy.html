Privacy Policy

DesiCalc is designed with privacy first. All calculators run entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any server.

What We Collect

Nothing. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All calculation inputs (property value, income, vehicle price, etc.) remain on your device and are never sent to our servers.

Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage solely to remember your language preference (English/Hinglish) and the last calculator inputs you entered, so you don't have to re-enter them on your next visit. This data never leaves your browser.

Third-Party Services

This site may display advertisements via third-party ad networks. These networks may use cookies and similar technologies to serve relevant ads. We do not control what data these networks collect. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for details.

We use Tailwind CSS via CDN for styling. The CDN provider may log standard HTTP request data (IP address, browser agent) as part of normal operations.

Analytics

We use minimal anonymous analytics to understand which calculators are most used. No personal identifiers are tracked. You can opt out of analytics tracking via your browser's Do Not Track setting.
