About DesiCalc

DesiCalc provides free, accurate financial calculators for Indian taxpayers and property buyers. No registration, no data upload — everything runs in your browser.

What We Offer

  • Stamp Duty Calculator — Calculate property registration charges for all 28 states including female concession, circle rate, and first-time buyer benefits.
  • RTO Road Tax Calculator — Compute vehicle registration tax for all states with accurate state-wise rates.
  • Income Tax Calculator — Estimate your tax under old and new regime for FY 2026-27.
  • Guides & Comparisons — Detailed guides on ITR filing, stamp duty, RTO registration, and tax regime comparisons.

Data Sources

All tax rates are sourced from respective state government publications, transport department notifications, and the Income Tax Department of India. Rates are updated for FY 2026-27. State stamp duty and RTO tax rates are verified against official government sources.

Disclaimer

The calculations provided are indicative and for reference only. Actual tax liability may vary based on specific circumstances, local circle rates, and government notifications. Please verify with your CA, tax consultant, or relevant government authority before making financial decisions.
